Electronics and Telecommunications Engg.

Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering is a well-known branch of engineering. It deals with the reception of video, microprocessors, analogue communication.

Computer Science Engg.

Computer Engineering combines the fields of electrical engineering and computer science to create new computer hardware and software.

Mechanical Engg.

Mechanical engineering is the study, design, development, construction, and testing of mechanical and thermal sensors and devices, including tools, engines, and machines.

Electrical Engg.

Electrical Engineering is that branch of Engineering which deals with the study of application of electricity, electronics and electromagnetism. Trained electrical engineers design electric circuits and equipment.

Civil Engg.

One of the oldest and broadest engineering disciplines, Civil Engineering involves planning, designing, constructing, maintaining and supervising infrastructures which include facilities essential to modern life like highways, bridges and tunnels.
